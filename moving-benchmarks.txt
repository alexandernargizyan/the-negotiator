# Moving Cost Benchmarks (FMCSA Data)

## Average Moving Costs 2025-2026

### Local Moves (Same State, <100 miles)
- 1 bedroom: $800 - $1,500
- 2 bedroom: $1,200 - $2,500
- 3 bedroom: $1,800 - $3,500
- 4+ bedroom: $2,500 - $5,000

### Long Distance Moves (100+ miles)
- 1 bedroom: $1,500 - $3,000
- 2 bedroom: $2,000 - $5,000
- 3 bedroom: $3,000 - $7,000
- 4+ bedroom: $5,000 - $10,000+

### Additional Fees
- Packing service: $200 - $800 per room
- Stairs fee (per flight): $75 - $250
- Elevator fee: $0 - $100
- Long carry distance: $50 - $200 (per 100 ft over 75 ft)
- Fuel surcharge: 10-15% of base price
- Insurance/valuation: $50 - $200 (basic)
- Peak season (May-Sept): +15-25%
- Shuttle service: $200 - $500
- Storage: $100 - $300/month

### Red Flags
- Price more than 30% below average: likely lowball with hidden fees
- Price more than 50% above average: likely overcharging
- No written estimate: required by FMCSA
- Demand for large deposit (>20%): potential scam
- No USDOT number: unregistered carrier

### FMCSA Requirements
- All interstate movers must have a USDOT number
- Must provide written estimate before moving
- Must provide "Your Rights and Responsibilities" booklet
- Arbitration program required
- No more than 110% of non-binding estimate without customer approval
